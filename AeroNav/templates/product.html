Know AeroNav

Discover the power of OpenCV Python for AI gesture recognition, a groundbreaking technology in human-computer interaction.

  • OpenCV: Open Source Computer Vision library for real-time computer vision.
  • Installation: Simply use pip install opencv-python in your Python environment.
  • Process: Capture video frame, convert to grayscale, apply Gaussian blur, thresholding, and contour detection.
  • AI Model: Train the model with hand gestures and corresponding outcomes for recognition.

Dive into the future of AI and gesture recognition with AeroNav!
